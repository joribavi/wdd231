Website Plan - WDD231 Project | Jonathan Villegas

Site Name

All for your Kids

The name represents a children's clothing shop that sells all kind of clothes for kids aged 0 to 15 years. In this case it is focused on clients all across Argentina

The domain will be: https://joribavi.github.io/wdd231/allforyour-kids.html

Site Purpose

The site provides product listing and information for parents and people looking for children clothes. Including a form to ask for available products to shop.

Scenarios

  • Where to find children's clothing and get it delivered to my home in Argentina?
  • How can I check product availability?
